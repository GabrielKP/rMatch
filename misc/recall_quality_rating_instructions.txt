The following are pairs of the participant's recall and the story. Rate how much the recall segment refers to the story segment from 0-6, where 0 indicates no relation, and 6 indicates full capture of the details in the story segment.

Normal segments
0 - information is not present in story segment (potentially: it's clear that this recall seg refers to the specific story seg but it doesn't capture much of note)
1 - very little information of story segment is captured (minimal capture)
2 - a few details from the story segment are captured
3 - 'half' of the information in story segment is present.
4 - more than half information is there, but still largely shy of fully capturing the details.
5 - almost all details captured, just minor information missing
6 - all details captured (need not be verbatim but captured)

Important Notes:
* Do not worry about excess information in the recall, only look at what overlaps
* Ignore incorrect information, only focus on the information that is overlapping, and how much overlaps
