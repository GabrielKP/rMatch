The following are pairs of the participant's recall and the story. Rate how much the recall segment refers to the story segment from 0-8, where 1 indicates no relation, and 9 indicates full capture of the details in the story segment.
If a recall segment is a summarizing statement, mark it as 'summary' and use the descriptions.
Compared to pure lack of information, in a summary information which the description implies, or plausibly contains the story-segment.


Normal segments
0 - information is not present in story segment
1 - single piece of information of story segment        / recall segment broadly summarizes this story segment
2 - a few details from the story segment                / recall segment summarizes story segment well.
3 - significant chunks from the story segment missing   / although summary, captures story segment with not too much ambiguity (?).
4 - 'half' of the information in story segment is present.
5 - more than half information is there, but also more than just a part missing.
6 - a part of the story segment missing.
7 - almost all details captured, just minor information missing (e.g. exact names)
8 - all details captured





Example 1:
* Story segment: Alice went running at sunset in New York.
* Recall segment: A character named alice went running in New York at sunset.
* Rating: 8
